Hello!

My name is Andy.
I'm a web designer that has currently graduated from Lambda School

Lambda School is an intense one year fullstack developer curriculum that simulates the type of environment you'll work in when you land that job. Every month I collaborated with other students on a group project using tools like Git, Trello, VScode, PhotoShop and Slack for daily standups with my team leader and my peers. Lambda is designed to follow a specific agenda designed so I will pick up new skills quickly. Individual assesments were built in to the program at every corner of my program allowing me to repeat a section until mastery has set in.

Some of the tech stacks I have worked with are JavaScript, NodeJS, Python, MongoBD, ExpressJS, SASS/LESS, and React.

When I'm not coding or thinking about coding, you can find me messing around in my garden, playing some pool or watching some Notre Dame Football.
