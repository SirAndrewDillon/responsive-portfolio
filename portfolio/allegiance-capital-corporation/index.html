Allegiance Capital Corporation

Web Design / Responsive Design / Web Development / Wordpress

Allegiance Capital Corporation, a premier private investment bank headquartered in Dallas, Texas, approached Lunarbot Studio to refresh their website. They wanted something that was similar to their existing website but with a fresh coat of paint. The new website needed to clearly communicate their extensive background and experience in the M & A industry, build trust and confidence in the user, and provide a platform to manage and create new content, as needed.

A new, custom Wordpress theme was designed with a responsive layout. Custom iconography were created to represent the various industries supported by Allegiance Capital. These icons were then used to tag various articles, pages and media to indicate what industry the content was related to.

The site was built on HTML5 standards and implemented into Wordpress for complete content management.
